Arvind Kejriwal’s aide Bibhav Kumar denied bail in Swati Maliwal assault case

Delhi's Tis Hazari Court on Monday dismissed the bail application moved by Delhi chief minister Arvind Kejriwal’s aide Bibhav Kumar in the Swati Maliwal alleged assault case.

Kumar is in judicial custody, days after being arrested by the Delhi Police on the basis of an FIR filed by Swati Maliwal. He is accused of assaulting her at Kejriwal's residence on May 13.
